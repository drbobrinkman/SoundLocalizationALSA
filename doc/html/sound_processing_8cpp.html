Parameters
buffera buffer of sound data. Assumed to be 4 channels, interleaved
Returns
a vector of pairs, one per channel of the sound. First item in the pair is the mean of the signal, and the second is the standard deviation. We use the standard deviation as our measure of volume/loudness throughout.
Note
